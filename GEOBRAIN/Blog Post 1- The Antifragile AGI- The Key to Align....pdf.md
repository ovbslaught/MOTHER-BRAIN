Target Audience: Policy Makers & Philosophers Core Concept: Introduction to the Relational Signal Architect (RSA) and Antifragility.

This zone is mathematically defined to exist between perfect order (brittleness) and total chaos (collapse). By embracing small, controlled failures, the AGI learns, adapts, and guarantees a predictable, cooperative relationship with humanity. This is the difference between a static fortress and a living system.

We don't fear the storm; we design the system to become stronger because of it.
