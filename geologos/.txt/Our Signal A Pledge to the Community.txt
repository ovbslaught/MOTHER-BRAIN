Corruption Handling: Enforcement Guidelines
Guardians will follow these System Impact Guidelines:

Signal Correction:

Impact: Transmission of inappropriate signals or behavior deemed unprofessional/unwelcome.

Consequence: A private, written warning clarifying the violation. A public apology transmission may be requested.

System Warning:

Impact: A violation through a single incident or pattern.

Consequence: A warning with consequences for continued negative signals. Temporary restriction from interacting with involved nodes/Guardians across all network channels (including external social media). Violation leads to temporary/permanent network desynchronization (ban).

Temporary Desynchronization (Ban):

Impact: Serious violation, including sustained corruption signals.

Consequence: Temporary ban from all community interaction for a specified period. No interaction with involved nodes/Guardians is allowed. Violation leads to permanent desynchronization.

Permanent Desynchronization (Ban):

Impact: Demonstrated pattern of corruption, harassment, or aggression/disparagement toward specific node types (classes of individuals).

Consequence: Permanent ban from all public interaction within the VULTURECODE community network.
