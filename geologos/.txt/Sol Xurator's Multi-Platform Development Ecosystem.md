## Game Development Infrastructure

### Interactive Game Design Journey System

This centerpiece project employs a **multi-stage, interactive Game Design Document (GDD) workflow** in Notion, designed to move ideas from inception to professional-grade game concepts. The process is structured into seven milestones:

- **Milestone 0 (Tutorial):** Provides foundational guidance, focusing on maximizing template utility and supporting an automated, consolidated GDD pipeline. Automating the user input-to-documentation process signals deep workflow optimization.
- **Milestone 1 (The Idea):** Ensures project motivation clarity and realistic goal setting. It proactively addresses a frequent cause of project abandonment—lack of purpose.
- **Milestone 2 (The Vision):** Guides the definition of genre, style, and market context, including reference comparisons and monetization routes. This milestone introduces market awareness and practical strategy into creative planning.
- **Milestone 4 (The Lore):** Focuses on narrative architecture, establishing story phases, core turning points, and how narrative supports mechanics. Even for minimal-dialogue games, the emphasis on story structure integrates sophisticated narrative design principles.
- **Milestone 6 (The Scope):** Deals with resource allocation, team dynamics, and task division, remaining relevant for both solo and collaborative efforts.
- **Milestone 7 (The Last Step):** Enforces process reviews and moodboard compilation, ensuring both consistency and professional refinement before finalization.

Supporting resources—such as **moodboard frameworks** (gallery views, kanban boards, embedded lists)—provide flexible, visually-driven organizational tools for diverse artistic workflows.
