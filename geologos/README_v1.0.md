# GEOLOGOS Universal Knowledge Catalog Bible

## Version 1.0.0 | Released November 16, 2025

**A comprehensive, open-source, ethically-grounded universal knowledge synthesis spanning from cosmology to homesteading, democratizing access to understanding across scientific, creative, and philosophical domains.**

---

## 🎯 Mission Statement

The Catalog Bible represents humanity's collective knowledge organized as an integrated, hierarchical, and infinitely expandable architecture. It bridges scientific rigor with humanistic wisdom, technical precision with philosophical depth, and academic scholarship with practical wisdom.

**Core Principles:**
- **Universal Access:** Free, open-source, non-proprietary
- **Intellectual Integrity:** Rigorous citations, peer-reviewed frameworks, falsifiable claims
- **Ethical Grounding:** Implications considered, justice-centered, community-benefiting
- **Cross-Disciplinary:** 18 pillars interconnected through mathematical, algorithmic, and conceptual linkages
- **Living Document:** Community-contributed, version-controlled, perpetually evolving

## 📚 Structure: 18 Pillars, 132+ Sections, 450,000+ Words

### Science Pillars (11)
1. **Cosmology & Deep Space** — Galaxy catalogs, dark matter/energy, CMB, gravitational waves
2. **Astronomy & Astrophysics** — Stars, exoplanets, stellar evolution, observational data
3. **Physics** (Classical, Quantum, Relativity) — Mechanics, thermodynamics, electromagnetism, QM interpretations, GR applications
4. **Chemistry & Materials Science** — Atomic structure, bonding, thermodynamics, materials at nanoscale
5. **Earth Systems & Atmosphere** — Atmospheric physics, climate, weather, biogeochemical cycles
6. **Oceanography & Marine Science** — Ocean circulation, marine biology, geochemistry, conservation
7. **Biology** (General & Specialized) — Molecular to organismal, genetics, evolution, ecology
8. **Medicine & Human Physiology** — Organ systems, metabolism, pathophysiology, pharmacology, genetics
9. **Mathematics Foundations** — Linear algebra, differential geometry, probability, statistics, differential equations
10. **Sound & Audio Systems** — Acoustics, signal processing, synthesis, music cognition, spatial audio
11. **Algorithms & Computational Frameworks** — Sorting, graphs, ML, cryptography, quantum algorithms, distributed systems

### Humanities Pillars (4)
12. **Music Theory & Composition** — Harmony, form, orchestration, contemporary techniques
13. **Visual Arts & Digital Design** — Composition, color, typography, UI/UX, animation, generative art
14. **History, Culture & Society** — World epochs, religions, social structures, anthropology, archaeology
15. **Philosophy, Ethics & Knowledge Systems** — Epistemology, metaphysics, ethics, logic, consciousness

### Applied Pillars (3)
16. **Human Domains** (Architecture, Sustainability, Homesteading) — Building systems, sustainable design, food production, energy systems
17. **Plugin Ecosystems & Free Software** — VST architecture, DAWs, synthesis, open-source audio/music tools
18. **Meta-Documentation & Archival** — Version control, reproducibility, data management, FAIR principles, licensing
