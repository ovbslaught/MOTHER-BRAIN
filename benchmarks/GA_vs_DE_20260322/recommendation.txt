RECOMMENDATION
--------------

► USE DE (Differential Evolution) for the next experiment.

DE wins 3/4 dimensions on your real NOMADZ data. On an imbalanced classification
landscape with mixed entity types (graph nodes, tasks, characters, telemetry), DE's
best1bin mutation navigates the log(C)/log(γ) space more reliably than GA crossover.

The performance difference is not statistically significant (p=0.1101).
The gap is directional but slim — both are viable choices.
